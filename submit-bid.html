Step 1 of 5 — Bid Document Receipt
Verify Your Bid Documents Receipt
Before submitting a bid, you must purchase the official Bid Documents from the SLSU Procurement Office and enter the reference number from your official receipt.
Bid Documents are available at the SLSU Procurement Office, Admin Building during office hours (8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Mon–Fri). The receipt reference number is printed on your Official Receipt (OR).
Each receipt number is valid for one bid submission only and is tied to a specific procurement project. Ensure your receipt corresponds to the project you intend to bid on.
Don't have a receipt? Visit the Procurement Office.
